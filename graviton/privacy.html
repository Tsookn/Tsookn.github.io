Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Graviton is a motion-based game developed by Tsook Nir LTD. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Data We Do Not Collect

Graviton does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not operate any server and we do not use analytics or tracking SDKs of our own.

Motion Sensors

Graviton uses your device's motion sensors (gyroscope and accelerometer) to control gameplay. This data is used exclusively on your device in real time and is never stored or transmitted.

Local Storage

The app stores your game settings and best times locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

Advertising

Graviton displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. Depending on your choice in the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt, AdMob may access the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) to personalize ads. If you decline, non-personalized ads are shown instead. For details on AdMob's data practices, see Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Third Parties

The only third-party service integrated by Graviton is Google AdMob for advertising. No other analytics, tracking, or marketing SDKs are used.

Children

Graviton is not directed at children under the age of 13. Because no personally identifying information is collected by the app itself, no such information is knowingly received from children.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: tlc@tsook.net
